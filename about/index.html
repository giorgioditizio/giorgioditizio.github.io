I am a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Fabio Massacci in the DISI Security Group at the University of Trento, Italy. Prior to that, I was a research fellow at the DISI. During my master's degree, I joined the Information Security and Cryptography Group at CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security in Saarbrücken, Germany, under the supervision of Dr. Robert Künnemann. I received my B.Sc. in information engineering from the University of Brescia, Italy, and my M.Sc. in computer science from the University of Trento in 2016 and 2018, respectively.


My main research interests lie within network and system security. In particular, I work on topics regarding vulnerability analysis, threat intelligence, and modeling of malicious activities on the Internet.
