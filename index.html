Hello!

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Trento, Italy in the DISI Security Group under the supervision of Prof. Fabio Massacci. Previous to that, I was a research fellow at the Department of Information Engineering and Computer Science. During my master's degree, I joined the Information Security and Cryptography Group of Prof. Michael Backes at CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security in Saarbrücken, Germany under the supervision of Dr. Robert Künnemann. I received my B.Sc. in information engineering from the University of Brescia, Italy and my M.Sc. in computer science from the University of Trento in 2016 and 2018, respectively.


My main research interests lie within network and system security. In particular, I work on topics regarding vulnerability analysis, malware analysis, and modeling of malicious activities on the Internet.
